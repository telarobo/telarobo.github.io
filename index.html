Our Advantage

We are the first company to bring teleoperation in the lawn mowing industry.

Reduced Operation Cost

Teleoperated control of any lawn mower at any location reduces the cost of flex and surge staffing.

Increased Productivity

Save travel time between different sites and use remote lawn mower operator to balance out surges in operational demand.

Enhanced Operator Safety

Move lawn mower operators from harsh environments by empowering them to work in remote locations up to thousands of miles away.

Reduce Labor Shortage

Hire from inaccessible labor pools such as the geographically isolated or physically impaired, and enhance labor retention by offering teleoperated positions.

AI Based Safety Features

AI based solutions are used to stop the mower when the operator is distracted.

Increased Fuel Efficiency

Reducing operator weight on lawn mower increases fuel efficiency by reducing fuel consumption.

Cutting-edge, real-time communications

Telarobo leverages existing network infrastructure to safely transmit data between widely spread endpoints using reliable, secure protocols and APIs. Telarobo also monitors end-to-end network health, so if any major network disruption takes place, the lawn mower will safely stop.

Low latency, high safety and precision

We provide a lawn mowing experience that exceeds the greatest standards, with end-to-end latency as low as 300ms using advanced IoT solutions. This combination of low latency, secure network streaming, and unmatched flexibility is unique.
